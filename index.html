Environmental Impact

Eco-friendly Stationery Solutions

The average office worker uses 10,000 sheets of copy paper each year. 45% of paper printed in offices ends up trashed by the end of the day.

Let me introduce you to the solution. Rewritable books offer the options of reusing the paper notebooks we usually use once and throw away. With the technology of "write it, then wipe it", these books can replace all one-time-use paper notebooks, and they last for years.

Our commitment to the environment has lead us to convert one full wing of an MNC to using rewritable books. With just this endeavor, Over 32 lakh litres of water were saved and Over 96,000 trees were preserved.
